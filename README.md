Clinical Readme: Helminth Parasite Treatments
=============================================

This repository contains professional clinical documentation and original medical journals focused on the naturopathic treatment of helminth parasites. This content is VirusTC-branded and reflects research collaborations involving USAMRICD, Fred Hutchinson Cancer Research, and Virus Treatment Centers.

Overview
--------

The provided materials outline diagnostic categories for human-parasitic helminths and research-backed naturopathic interventions. These resources are designed for professionals across pathology, agriculture, and medicine to explore integrative deworming protocols that minimize the use of unstable chemicals.

### Key Clinical Areas

-   **Pathology**: Detailed classification of helminth groups, including flatworms (platyhelminths), thorny-headed worms (acanthocephalins), and roundworms (nematodes).

-   **Agriculture**: Research into how environmental conditions, such as soil pH, affect the molecular potency of botanical treatments like *Azadirachta* (Neem).

-   **Medicine**: Evidence-based prescriptions for botanical compounds intended to inhibit helminth growth and break down parasite biology without the bone or cellular damage associated with traditional deworming chemicals.
