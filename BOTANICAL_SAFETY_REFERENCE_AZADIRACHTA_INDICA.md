# BOTANICAL SAFETY INFORMATION: *Azadirachta indica* (Neem)

## 1. Document Purpose
This document provides educational information regarding the botanical properties and known safety risks associated with *Azadirachta indica*. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

## 2. Documented Safety Risks and Contraindications
The use of *Azadirachta indica* derivatives requires significant caution due to the following documented risks:

* **Systemic Toxicity:** Concentrated extracts contain potent bioactive compounds. Ingestion of unrefined neem oil has been associated with toxic effects, including metabolic acidosis and encephalopathy.
* **Organ Stress:** High doses or prolonged exposure can lead to acute hepatic (liver) distress and severe gastrointestinal irritation.
* **Vulnerable Populations:** Products derived from *Azadirachta indica* are strictly contraindicated for infants and children. There are documented cases of severe reactions in pediatric patients, including Reye's-like syndrome. It should also be avoided during pregnancy and breastfeeding due to potential reproductive toxicity.

## 3. Professional Consultation
Before using any botanical extract for health-related purposes, it is essential to consult with a qualified healthcare professional or a licensed toxicologist to understand potential interactions with other medications and specific health conditions.
